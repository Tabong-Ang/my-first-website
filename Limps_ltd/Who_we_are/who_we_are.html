About Us

LIMPS is a social innovation company registered in the Republic of cameroon. We are a cooperation of passion driven and enthusiastic youths who share a common vision in effecting positive change and revitalizing youthful potentials through leadership, empowermen, counselling, development, cooperation, and international youth symposium program.

Our vision

To effect positive change and revitalize youthful potential

Our Mission

To bring out the great potentials in today's leaders inorder fo them to enjoy a divine purposeful life
